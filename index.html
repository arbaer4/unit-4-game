Crystals Collector

Rules

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game
  • There are four crystals below, each with a hidden value
  • By clicking on a crystal you will add their specific value to your total score
  • You win the game by matching your total score to the random number
  • You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number
  • Each time the game starts, the values of each crystal will be different
